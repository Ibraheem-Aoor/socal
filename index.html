Our Services

At SOCAL PETROLEUM, we provide a wide range of high-quality oil and lubricant solutions designed to meet the diverse needs of industries worldwide. Our services are backed by cutting-edge technology, ensuring reliability and sustainability in every product we deliver.

Engine Oil

Our engine oils are crafted to ensure optimal performance in combustion engines by reducing friction, minimizing wear, and maintaining engine temperature. By using the right oil for your engine, you extend its lifespan and enhance efficiency.

High-Temperature Grease Products

Our high-temperature grease products offer superior protection against rust, corrosion, and extreme temperatures. Made from premium base oils and rust-resistant agents, they provide long-lasting lubrication for both ferrous and non-ferrous metals.

Mineral Oils

Our mineral oils serve a variety of purposes, from heat conduction to hydraulic drives in cranes and presses. In addition, lighter types are utilized in the manufacturing of fabrics and textiles, providing versatile solutions for industrial needs.
